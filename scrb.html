Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21 May 2026

Welcome to SCRB 2 Post. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Facebook and Instagram posting services.

1. Information We Collect

SCRB 2 Post may collect the following information:

  • Facebook account basic profile information
  • Instagram account information
  • Access tokens provided through Meta OAuth login
  • Page and account IDs
  • Content you choose to publish
  • App usage logs for debugging and security

We do not collect passwords for your Facebook or Instagram accounts.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information only for:

  • Posting content to Facebook Pages
  • Posting content to Instagram accounts
  • Managing connected social media accounts
  • Improving app functionality
  • Security and fraud prevention

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your data may only be shared:

  • With Meta APIs (Facebook & Instagram)
  • When required by law
  • To protect platform security

4. Data Storage

Access tokens and account information may be stored securely to provide continuous service.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

5. User Control

You may:

  • Disconnect your Facebook or Instagram account anytime
  • Revoke app permissions from Facebook settings
  • Request deletion of stored data
